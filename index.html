About Football Manager 2023

Football Manager 2023 is a simulation game that allows players to manage a football team. Players can select a team to manage and then make decisions such as buying and selling players, setting tactics, and competing in matches. The goal is to lead the team to success by winning matches and competing in competitions.

Features of Football Manager 2023

  • Realistic player and team dynamics
  • Tactical customization
  • Comprehensive transfer and contract negotiations
  • Dynamic and immersive match day experience
